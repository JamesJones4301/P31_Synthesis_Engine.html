Equipment

Running route or treadmill, dumbbells, loaded backpack, towel or band, sturdy step, open floor, optional medicine ball.

Rules

Warm up first. Keep form strict. Scale load before you scale effort. Stop for pain, dizziness, chest pain, or unusual shortness of breath.

HYROX criteria used

HYROX races use 8 x 1 km runs with 8 stations in order: SkiErg, sled push, sled pull, burpee broad jumps, row, farmer carry, sandbag lunges, wall balls. This home plan trains those patterns with dumbbell high pulls, loaded squat drives, towel pulls, burpee broad jumps, deadlifts, farmer carries, backpack lunges, and thrusters.
